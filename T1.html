Chocobo Farm Short

This is the 1st Track in the Game. It takes place in the Daylight on a Farm/Village like location. It is a very simple Track with only a couple moving tractors towards the end of the track.This track is also great for learning the feeling of how the game works.

For Time Attack, the best character to use for this track is Odin in my opinion for both Beginner and Master Speed, however I highly recommend to start out with Chocobo and then switch as you learn the mechanics better.

Difficulty

Staff Ghosts
